VR/Metaverse Space Quarters

This package works only in Unity version 2020.2.2f1 and higher.
If you need HDRP or URP version click on 'VR/Metaverse Space Quarters (HDRP).unitypackage' or 'VR/Metaverse Space Quarters (URP).unitypackage' in HDRP_URP_packages folder.

VR/Metaverse Space Quarters environment with well designed, optimised, detailed interior, and one HDRI with stars and a planet.
This environment was ment for VR, but it can be used in any project.
